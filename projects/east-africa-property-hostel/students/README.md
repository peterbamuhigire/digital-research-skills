## Status

Wave 1 + Wave 2 complete. ~90 sources.

## Top findings

- **80% of Ugandan tenants never recover their deposits** despite the 2022 Act mandating 30-day refund — the sharpest single statistic in the entire engine
- UoN water shortages run for months (Jan–Mar 2026)
- Bedbugs at **58% prevalence** in Tanzanian boarding-school study (n=384, Moshi 2020)
- UR Huye 2025 — students slept in the **university gymnasium** when ~6,000 newcomers exceeded the ~3,000-bed capacity
- **University of Juba (June 2024):** police raid on female hostel — 23 students arrested, ~16 injured

See `research/complaints-report.md` for the full corpus.

## Reports rolled into this sub-project

This sub-project covers two perspectives at once: **finding** a hostel, and **living** in one. Both are the student's perspective. The supply-side perspective (hostel owners) lives in the sibling `owners/` sub-project.
