# CLAUDE.md — students sub-project

Sub-project-specific notes. Inherits all rules from the project `CLAUDE.md` and engine root `CLAUDE.md`.

## Cohort definition

University students (undergraduate + postgraduate) in East Africa, specifically in their role as **prospective and current hostel residents**. Includes:

- Government-sponsored students (HELB Kenya, HESLB Tanzania, Uganda allowances)
- Private / self-sponsored students
- First-years, returning students, finalists
- Day scholars who use hostels as occasional accommodation

## Out of scope

- Hostel owner perspective (lives in sibling `owners/`)
- Non-student tenants (lives in sibling `tenants/`)
- LGBTQ+ topics — engine-wide exclusion

## Wave history

- **Wave 1** (initial): broad sweep, ~50 sources. Covered: scams, capacity shortage, sanitation, water/power, bedbugs, deposit theft, unfair eviction, named hostels (Pearl View, Cotilde, Olympia, Paradise/Madonna/Bavana, New Nana).
- **Wave 2 (gap-fill)**: South Sudan + Burundi, Reddit attempt (constrained), Makerere 2023 inspection, mental-health correlations, female-specific harm, named-hostel inventory expansion, court-records search. Added ~40 new sources.
