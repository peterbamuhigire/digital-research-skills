# Raw student & stakeholder quotes

Verbatim quotes from public sources — student voices first, then media/stakeholder voices. Use these for empathy maps, persona work, and product copy.

## Sanitation

> "The sanitation at the hostel is very poor. Sewage is everywhere. It's worse in the compound, near the reading rooms and the canteen."
— Pearl View Hostel resident, Makerere area
[source](https://dorothynakamyuka.wordpress.com/2014/03/14/makerere-pearl-view-hostel-sudents-decry-poor-sanitation/)

## Bedbugs & hygiene

> "After barely a month in residence I began to feel painful itchy bites at night... Even when I sprayed, it didn't help because people kept bringing them back. The infestation spread amongst rooms and visitors. This discomfort exposed deeper issues — poor hygiene, overcrowding and weak management."
— Joshua Arinda, Makerere student
[source](https://campusbee.ug/lifestyle/hostels/hostel-review-list-and-costs-mak-hostels/)

## Water shortages

> "Water shortages have turned hostel life into a daily struggle, reshaping the university experience into one of endurance rather than learning."
— University of Nairobi student account, March 2026
[source](https://www.the-star.co.ke/news/2026-03-27-my-struggle-with-water-shortages-at-university-of-nairobi-hostels)

## Management disputes

> "I have been in this hostel for two years now paying my bills on time and one time when their own management makes a mistake I am to pay more money for it. I am so hurt."
— Olympia Hostel review, Kikoni

## Overcrowding (Kyambogo)
