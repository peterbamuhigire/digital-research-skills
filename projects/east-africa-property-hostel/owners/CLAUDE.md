# CLAUDE.md — owners sub-project

Sub-project-specific notes. Inherits all rules from the project `CLAUDE.md` and engine root `CLAUDE.md`.

## Cohort definition

Owners and managers of **student hostels** specifically. Includes:

- Single-property owner-operators (most common in UG / KE / TZ)
- Multi-property family operators
- Institutional operators (Acorn Holdings is the named benchmark)
- University-affiliated hostel managers (Makerere halls, Kenyatta U PPP)
- Faith-based / mission-affiliated hostel operators (mentioned, lightly mapped)

## Out of scope

- Student tenant perspective (lives in `students/`)
- General residential landlord (lives in `landlords/`)
- Hotel / Airbnb operators
- LGBTQ+ topics — engine-wide exclusion
