## Synthesis

The owner voice is **quieter** than the student voice in public sources — there is no "Campus Bee for landlords," no consumer-facing forum where owners aggregate complaints. The pain is documented through:
- Their actions (evicting MUBS students, opposing fee guidelines)
- Press write-ups quoting them indirectly
- Industry analysts inferring their constraints from market data

This silence is itself a finding. A trusted owner community / forum may be the most underexploited content opportunity in the entire space.
