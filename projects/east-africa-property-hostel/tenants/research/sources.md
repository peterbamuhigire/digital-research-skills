## Specific articles / data points cited in main report

- **Daily Monitor Aug 2023** — 80% deposit non-refund Uganda
- **Spectrum Real Estate Solutions Uganda** — 30% fake-listing scam encounter
- **Medium / Daily Nation research** — 90% single-women rejection vs single men
- **NCWSC** — 250M-litre / day Nairobi water deficit
- **NWSC** — 60M-litre / day Kampala shortage
- **DAWASA** — 9 hours / day Dar supply; 50% system loss
- **IGC / Bloomberg 2024** — Kigali 79% informal settlements; 54% earn $38–225/mo
- **2024 Nairobi riparian demolitions** — 20,000+ displaced; KSh 10,000 compensation
- **Mathare Valley study** — 87% earn below minimum wage; 80.9% in rent arrears
