# Tenant-side theme taxonomy

Three top-level categories: **getting in** (search and signing), **living in** (day-to-day), and **getting out** (eviction, deposit, dispute). Each tagged by prevalence and severity.

## A. Getting in

| # | Sub-theme | Prevalence | Severity | Key evidence |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| A1 | Broker viewing-fee extortion | Universal | Medium | KSh 500–2,000 KE; UGX 10,000+ UG per property |
| A2 | Fake listings / photo theft | High (UG 30%) | High | Spectrum Real Estate Solutions UG |
| A3 | Multi-deposit collection scam | High | Critical when triggered | Same unit rented to multiple tenants |
| A4 | "Holding fee" theft | High | Medium | Broker collects, disappears |
| A5 | Discrimination at letting | Universal | High | Single women 90% rejection; refugees, PWDs |
| A6 | House-hunting fatigue | Universal | Medium | 6–12 viewings before signing; cumulative cost ≥1 month rent |
| A7 | Bait-and-switch on online platforms | High | Medium | Property24, BuyRent listing inaccuracy |
| A8 | No written lease (UG below UGX 500k threshold) | High | High | Statutory protections kick in only with documentation |

## B. Living in

| # | Sub-theme | Prevalence | Severity | Key evidence |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| B1 | Arbitrary mid-lease rent hikes | Universal | High | Statutory caps (UG 10%, KE inflation-cap proposed) ignored |

| B5 | Power outages | Universal | High | KPLC / UMEME / TANESCO 2–4×/month |
| B6 | Internet quality | Universal | Medium | Shared GPON 32–64 households per fiber |

| B8 | Caretaker bribery | High | Medium-High | Tenant pays rent + side payment for repairs |
| B9 | Caretaker harassment / unlawful entry | High | Critical when triggered | Documented sexual-favour requests (Daily Nation) |
| B10 | Caretaker espionage to landlord | High | Medium | Privacy violations |
| B11 | KPLC token scams | High in Kenya | Medium | Tenants pay for tokens that don't load |
| B12 | No rent-abatement when service falls | Universal | High | No EA jurisdiction provides this remedy |
| B13 | Construction-cycle / election-cycle hikes | High | Medium-High | 10–25% jumps documented |
| B14 | Bar / pastor / NGO conversion in residential block | High in KE / UG | Medium | Noise, security, no remedy for tenants |

## C. Getting out

| # | Sub-theme | Prevalence | Severity | Key evidence |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| C1 | Deposit non-refund | Critical (UG 80%) | Critical | Daily Monitor 2023 |
| C2 | Vague "damages" / "maintenance" deductions | Universal | High | Itemised deductions rare |
| C3 | Landlord disappears at lease end | High | High | Anecdotal; widespread |
| C4 | Self-help eviction (lockout, furniture removal) | Universal despite UG 2022 ban | Critical | 3-month-rent damages remedy unused |
| C5 | Power / water cut as eviction tool | High | Critical | Tenants can't file fast enough |
| C6 | Court-process speed | Universal | High | KE 7–11m, UG 4–18m, TZ 8–12m, RW 3–5m |
| C7 | Riparian / road-project demolitions | High | Critical (mass) | Nairobi 2024: 20,000+ displaced, KSh 10k compensation |
| C8 | Election-cycle mass evictions | Episodic | Critical | Nairobi 2022, Kampala 2021 |
| C9 | Holdover-tenant disputes | High | Medium | Tenant stays past notice; landlord limited options |
| C10 | "Eating the deposit" tenant counter-strategy | High | Risk to tenant | Withholding final month's rent — landlord can sue for both |

## D. Cross-cutting

| # | Sub-theme | Prevalence | Severity | Key evidence |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| D1 | Awareness gap (<30% know rights) | Universal | Critical structural | Statute exists, ignored |
| D2 | No tenant union | Universal | Critical structural | Asymmetry vs landlord WhatsApp networks |
| D3 | Legal-aid scarcity | Universal | High | Hakijamii, Justice Centres reach minority |
| D4 | Constitutional protections unenforced (KE Art. 27, UG 21/42) | Universal | High | <10% case enforcement |

## Severity-weighted top 5 for product strategy

1. **C1 — Deposit theft** (universal, critical, recurring, quantified at 80% UG)
2. **A2 + A3 + A4 — Listing / broker fraud** (universal, high, addressable via verification)
3. **B4 + B12 — Utility failure with no remedy** (universal, high, rent-abatement gap)
4. **A5 — Discrimination at letting** (universal, high, requires data + collective bargaining)
5. **C4 + C5 — Self-help eviction** (universal despite ban, critical)

## What's not yet mapped

- **Quantitative tenant satisfaction surveys** at scale (academic gap)
- **Caretaker-misconduct prevalence** beyond anecdote
- **Refugee-tenant household-level data**
- **Disability-housing accessibility hard numbers**
- **Tenant payment-on-time data linked to landlord behaviour** (would feed a credit-bureau product)
- **Reddit verbatim corpus** — TOS-constrained
- **"Eating the deposit" counter-strategy litigation outcomes**
