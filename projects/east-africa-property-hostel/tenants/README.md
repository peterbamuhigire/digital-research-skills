## Top findings

- **80% of Ugandan tenants never recover deposits** despite 2022 Act mandating 30-day refund — same statistic that hits the `students/` cohort
- **~90% landlord rejection rate for single women** vs single men of similar income (single source — flagged in `EVIDENCE-AUDIT.md` for verification)
- **30% of Ugandan renters encounter fake listings** (Spectrum Real Estate Solutions)
- **Nairobi 250M-litre/day water deficit** (NCWSC); Kampala 60M; Dar 9hr/day supply
- **2024 Nairobi riparian demolitions** — 20,000+ displaced from Kibera/Mathare/Mukuru with KSh 10,000 compensation each
- **Mathare benchmark** — 87% earn below minimum wage; 80.9% in rent arrears (Huchzermeyer et al, 2020)
- **Awareness gap** — fewer than 30% of tenants know their statutory rights — the largest single structural gap

See `research/pain-points-report.md` for the full corpus.
