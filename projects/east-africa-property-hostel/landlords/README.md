## Top findings

- **Eviction delays:** 7–11 months Kenya, 4–18 months Uganda, 8–12 months Tanzania, 3–5 months Rwanda (fastest in EA)
- **CRB adoption <5%** for tenant screening despite Metropol / TransUnion / Creditinfo all available
- **Mortgage penetration only 3.6% in Kenya** — lowest in EA
- **Currency risk** — KSh -26.8% vs USD pushed material costs +30–50%
- **Two new 2024–25 court rulings** make deposit-refund claims faster: Tribunal Case E007/2024 (60-day rule under CAP 301) and Muhanda v LP Holdings (2025) (Small Claims Court jurisdiction)
- **Tanzania *dalali* market unregulated** — Nov 2021 broker-fee ban not enforced; March 2025 regulatory body announced
- **Largest unfilled product gap:** vertically integrated landlord OS for 5–50 unit segment (M-Pesa rent + ledger + screening + maintenance + statutory reminders)
