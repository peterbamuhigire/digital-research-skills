# CLAUDE.md — landlords sub-project

Sub-project-specific notes. Inherits all rules from the project `CLAUDE.md` and engine root `CLAUDE.md`.

## Cohort definition

Owners and managers of **general residential rental properties** — bungalows, apartments, flats, "self-contained" units, single rooms / *muzigos*, small unit blocks (4–20 doors), mid-tier complexes, commercial-residential mixes.

## Out of scope

- Student hostels (lives in sibling `owners/`)
- Tenant perspective (lives in sibling `tenants/`)
- Commercial-only buildings, hotels, short-let / Airbnb (mention briefly only)
- LGBTQ+ topics — engine-wide exclusion

## Wave history

- **Wave 1** — finding tenants, screening, rent collection, defaults, maintenance, regulation, financing, disputes, problematic-tenant categories. ~60 sources.

## Project-specific watch-outs

- **Eviction-time figures** (7–11 months KE, 4–18m UG, etc.) are anecdotal compositions across multiple journalism sources — not from a single court-statistics dataset. Mark as "(synthesised — no single authoritative source)" in the final report.
- **CRB landlord-adoption "<5%"** is industry estimate; not a measured number. Mark as "(estimated)".
- **Default-rate "10–25% in informal segments"** is anecdotal. Mark as "(anecdotal)" or strike from the final report unless a published study can be located.
