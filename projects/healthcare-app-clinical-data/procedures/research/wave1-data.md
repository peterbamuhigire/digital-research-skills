## Notes on Data Completeness

### Gaps by Field

**ICHI code gaps:**
The majority of procedures lack stable ICHI codes (current release is beta, finalized only for clinical/functioning intervention component; many surgical codes remain under review). Wave 2 will reconcile as ICHI stabilizes post-2026. **ICD-10-PCS code gaps:** A small number of procedures (burn management, resuscitation, audiology) do not have direct ICD-10-PCS codes; these are marked as gaps and flagged for Wave 2 clarification. **CDT code gaps:** Full CDT roster could not be extracted due to ADA proprietary restriction; sample codes provided under fair-use. App team with ADA license will complete full roster in Phase 2.

### Connectivity tolerance, paper_form_equivalent, coding_rule

These fields are marked `[GAP — no source found]` across the dataset. While the design spec calls for these, sources (Uganda MoH guidelines, WHO documents, academic literature) do not explicitly detail:
- Connectivity tolerance thresholds (offline-first, periodic sync, etc.)
- Paper form equivalent templates (blank forms not provided in sources)
- Coding rules (implementation guidance for each procedure)

Wave 2 research will target Uganda MoH service delivery manuals and digital health guidelines for these implementation details.

### Source tier distribution

The table above uses primarily **T1 sources** (ICD-10-PCS, CDT, Uganda clinical guidelines, WHO standards). **T2 sources** (regional studies, specialist literature) triangulate for procedural volumes and level-of-care mappings. **T3 sources** (hospital directories, generic medical references) fill minor gaps only.
