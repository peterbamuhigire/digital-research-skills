**Total new rows (Wave 2):** 106 rows

**Status:** **SHORT OF TARGET** — needed ≥220 distinct tests; current 144 is 65% of target. Explanation: LOINC codes are granular; many test variants (e.g., hemoglobin by different methodologies/specimens) share the same LOINC code. To reach 220 distinct codes requires:
1. Additional variants in histopathology (H&E, IHC, electron microscopy techniques)
2. Molecular oncology (FISH, karyotype, mutation detection — BCR-ABL, HER2, BRAF, EGFR variants)
3. Rare enzyme/metabolic disorders (additional endocrinology panel variants)
4. Regional pathogen-specific tests (Ebola, Marburg, chikungunya; deferred per exclusion discipline)

**Wave 3 recommendation:** Extend into specialized lab domains (advanced immunology, metabolic disorders, genetic testing) with explicit HC-level gating (NRH/RRH only).
