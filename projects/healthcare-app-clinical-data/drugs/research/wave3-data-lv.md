## Notes on Wave 3 Strategy

**Gaps identified in Wave 1+2:**
- L01 (antineoplastics): only 42/~80+ drugs in WHO EML covered → expand platinum agents, taxanes, monoclonal antibodies, kinase inhibitors
- M (musculoskeletal): only 10/~60+ NSAIDs + topicals + gout → fill M01A NSAIDs (acetic acids, propionic, oxicams), M02 topicals, M04 gout, M05 bone disease
- N (nervous system): 42 covered; expand N03 full antiepileptic roster, N05 anxiolytics/hypnotics (full benzodiazepine+Z-drugs), N06 antidepressants (SSRIs, SNRIs, TCA, MAOI roster)

- R (respiratory): only 7 covered; expand R03 (SABA/LABA/ICS/LAMA full roster), R05 cough/cold, R06 antihistamines
- S01 (ophthalmologicals): only 4 covered; expand antibiotics, glaucoma agents, NSAIDs, mydriatics, anaesthetics
