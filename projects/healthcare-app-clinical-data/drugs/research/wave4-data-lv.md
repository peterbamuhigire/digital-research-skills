## Notes on Wave 4 Coverage

**Gaps from W1–W3 to close:**
- L01: kinase inhibitors (gefitinib, imatinib, dasatinib, nilotinib, sorafenib, sunitinib), additional platinum agents (nedaplatin), additional taxanes, topoisomerase inhibitors (irinotecan), protein kinase C inhibitors (midostaurin)
- L02: additional hormone therapies (bicalutamide, flutamide, goserelin)
- L04: calcineurin inhibitors (cyclosporine, tacrolimus), mTOR inhibitor (sirolimus), TNF-α inhibitors
- M01: additional NSAIDs (oxicams, acetic acids) and topicals
- M02–M05: bone diseases, gout, muscle relaxants (missing: tizanidine, orphenadrine, methocarbamol)
- N03: missing antiepileptics (gabapentin, pregabalin, topiramate, oxcarbazepine)
- N05: benzodiazepines (expanded roster), Z-drugs (zopiclone, zaleplon), antipsychotics (amisulpride, aripiprazole, paliperidone, quetiapine)
- N06: antidepressants (full SSRI/SNRI/MAOI roster), psychostimulants
- P01: antiprotozoals (pentamidine, atovaquone, primaquine, tafenoquine), trypanosomiasis drugs, leishmaniasis agents
- R03–R06: respiratory (SABA/LABA/ICS full set, antihistamines, antitussives)
- S01–S03: ophthalmologicals, otologic, dermatologic (topical antibiotics, NSAIDs, glaucoma agents, mydriatics)
- V01–V09: antidotes (specifics), diagnostics, gases, contrast media, radiopharmaceuticals
