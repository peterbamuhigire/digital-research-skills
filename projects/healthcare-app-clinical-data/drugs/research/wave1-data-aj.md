## Data Model Notes

**Column definitions (in order):**

- `atc_code` — 5-level ATC code (e.g., A02BC01)
- `atc_level1` — A, B, C, D, G, H, or J
- `atc_level2` — 2-digit therapeutic subgroup
- `atc_level3` — 1 letter, pharmacological group
- `atc_level4` — 1 letter, chemical subgroup
- `atc_ddd_value` — numeric Defined Daily Dose (per WHO ATC/DDD Index)
- `atc_ddd_unit` — unit of measurement (mg, g, μg, IU, drops, etc.)
- `rxnorm_rxcui` — RxNorm concept unique identifier; [GAP] if not found
- `inn` — International Nonproprietary Name
- `who_eml_inclusion` — TRUE (on EML 23), FALSE, or [GAP]
- `who_eml_section` — WHO EML section name/number; [GAP] if not confirmed
- `emhslu_inclusion` — TRUE (on EMHSLU 2023), FALSE, or [GAP]
- `emhslu_vital_essential_necessary` — V, E, N, or [GAP]
- `emhslu_level_of_care` — HC II, HC III, HC IV, GH, RRH, NRH, or [GAP]
- `dosage_forms` — pipe-separated list
- `strengths` — pipe-separated list
- `population` — "adult", "paediatric", "all-ages", or [GAP]
- `paeds_dose_summary` — paediatric dose or [GAP]
- `adult_dose_summary` — adult dose range
- `key_indications` — pipe-separated clinical uses
- `brand_inn` — INN for branded version
- `brand_1` | `brand_1_holder` — registered brand | manufacturer
- `brand_2` | `brand_2_holder` — second brand | holder
- `brand_3` | `brand_3_holder` — third brand | holder
- `brand_4` | `brand_4_holder` — fourth brand | holder
- `registered_uganda_nda` — TRUE, FALSE, [unverified], or [GAP]
- `registered_kenya_ppb` — TRUE, FALSE, [unverified], or [GAP]
- `registered_tanzania_tmda` — TRUE, FALSE, [unverified], or [GAP]
- `controlled_substance_schedule` — Schedule I–V or [GAP]
- `storage` — storage conditions
- `lasa_tallman_form` — tall-man lettering per ISMP or [GAP]
- `eml_tier` — "core", "complementary", or [GAP]
- `level_of_care_min` — HC II through NRH
- `cadre_min` — minimum cadre (CHW, Nurse, Clinical Officer, Medical Officer, Specialist, Pharmacist)
- `code_system_version` — ATC version (e.g., "ATC/DDD 2024")
- `code_accessed_date` — verification date
- `connectivity_tolerance` — "online", "offline", or [GAP]
- `paper_form_equivalent` — HMIS form reference or [GAP]
- `coding_rule` — additional coding rules or [GAP]
- `source_tier` — T1, T2, or T3
- `source_citation` — BibTeX key(s)
