# business-report-typology

Operational distillation of Dorinda Clippinger, *Business Report Guides: Research Reports and Business Plans*. Per-type templates for formal research reports and business plans, plus the wider report typology (informational/analytical/recommendation/progress/feasibility/justification/audit), three-way discipline (findings vs conclusions vs recommendations), and front-/back-matter rules.

See `SKILL.md`.
