# Claude-specific — business-report-typology

- Pick the report type before drafting; refuse to mix types.
- Apply the three-way discipline: findings (descriptive), conclusions (interpretive), recommendations (imperative + owned + dated + costed).
- Methodology must be replicable — instrument, sample, dates required.
- Scale front/back matter to length and formality; do not inflate.
- For business plans, require sensitivity analysis on financials.
