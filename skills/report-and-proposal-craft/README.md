# report-and-proposal-craft

Single entry skill for long-form business persuasion: business reports (informational, analytical, recommendation, progress, feasibility, audit, formal research), business plans, formal proposals (internal and external, RFP responses), and B2B white papers (backgrounder, numbered list, problem/solution). Artifact router, audience grid, X/Y/Z purpose statement, SCQA persuasion spine, three-way discipline, three `references/` (Forsyth, Clippinger, Graham).

For shorter prose forms, see `business-writing`.

See `SKILL.md`.
