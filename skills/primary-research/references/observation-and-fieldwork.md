# Observation and fieldwork

## What to capture

- Setting and actors
- Sequence of actions
- Bottlenecks, workarounds, interruptions
- Artifacts used
- Verbatim phrases when possible

## Rules

- Separate description from interpretation.
- Time-stamp key moments where possible.
- Record what was not observable, not just what was.
- Do not let one dramatic moment dominate the entire account.
