# Credibility and quality

## Minimum credibility checks

- Triangulate with at least one other source stream where possible.
- Surface minority or conflicting accounts.
- State sample limits honestly.
- Where appropriate, confirm interpretations with participants or domain experts.

## What strong qualitative rigor looks like

- Transparent sampling
- Coherent chain from raw material to theme to conclusion
- Explicit treatment of exceptions
- Useful, actionable reporting rather than decorative quotations
