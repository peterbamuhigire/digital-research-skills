# Claude-specific — analytic-tradecraft

- **Trigger.** Invoke this skill on any output containing a forward-looking judgment, a contested-evidence call, an "assess / conclude / estimate" statement, or a single-source load-bearing claim. Also invoke when synthesising findings from a research wave into a recommendation.
- **Read order.** SKILL.md first; then the references the decision router specifies. ICD 203 reference is the universal floor.
- **Hard rule.** Forward-looking confidence ships with a Kent / ODNI lexicon term + numeric band. "High confidence" alone is not enough.
- **Hard rule.** Probability of the judgment and confidence in the source are stated separately. The Iraq WMD NIE failure mode is structural; do not repeat it.
- **Hard rule.** Every load-bearing judgment carries an alternative hypothesis (even if rejected) and a list of the load-bearing assumptions challenged.
- **Hard rule.** Cognitive-bias audit is mandatory before ship: name the biases the conclusion is most exposed to; name the mitigation applied.
- **Refuse to embellish.** When evidence is thin, the judgment ships at LOW confidence with explicit indicators of what would corroborate. Do not upgrade confidence to satisfy a reader's preference for certainty.
- **Coordination is not word-smithing.** When restructuring an estimative judgment for executive presentation (`executive-communication`), the Kent lexicon and the dissent footnotes survive. Do not smooth them away.
