# Productization and monetization

## Asset ladder

1. Insight note
2. Internal framework
3. Repeatable report template
4. Workshop / advisory offer
5. Subscription update or dashboard
6. Long-form publication

## Rules

- Package the smallest useful unit first.
- Tie every offer to a real audience pain or decision.
- Keep methodology explicit; trust is part of the product.
- Refresh assets when the evidence base moves.

## Growth and profit product patterns

Use `../../../extracted-books/growth-profit-disruption-research-notes.txt` when a research asset can become a commercial decision aid. Strong product candidates include:

- growth engine diagnostic;
- revenue leak audit;
- product-led growth scorecard;
- pricing and packaging experiment plan;
- disruption readiness assessment;
- customer retention dashboard;
- growth experiment backlog.

For each product, define audience, painful decision, data required, metric or score produced, decision enabled, refresh cadence, and follow-on advisory, workshop, dashboard, or subscription offer.
