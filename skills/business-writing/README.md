# business-writing

Single entry skill for non-academic prose: emails, memos, letters, plans, blog articles, web copy, internal comms, fundraising, speeches, resumes, plus pitch decks, one-pagers, training material, business plans, grant proposals, performance appraisals, hard-scenario artefacts, and AI-assisted drafts of any of the above.

Channel router, seven-step process (compresses to Garner / Flowers MACJ), "don't mumble" master rule, and nine `references/`:

- `maxwell-7-steps.md` — process spine; MACJ four-task compression; accordion method
- `roman-raphaelson-principles.md` — sentence-level discipline; HBR / Garner bizspeak blacklist; tone, chronology, bad-news letter pattern, performance-appraisal scaffolding
- `persuasion-and-narrative.md` — Hood / Shiach / Geffner / Rubie & Provost; Lindsell-Roberts four pillars and story arc; Henwood story-vs-argument distinction
- `web-copywriting.md` — F-pattern, scannability, landing pages
- `blog-publishing-workflow.md` — full blog article workflow
- `language-standards-en-fr-sw.md` — English (British / East African), French (Francophone African), Kiswahili (East African)
- `ai-assisted-writing.md` — Lindsell-Roberts WI/AI division, prompt design, evidence-discipline overlay
- `scenario-playbooks.md` — Ramsey four-axis frame, scenario library, twelve negative-news pitfalls
- `extended-channels.md` — Henwood / Lindsell-Roberts / Garner channels: pitch decks, one-pagers, training material, business plans, grant proposals, performance appraisals

For reports, proposals, and white papers, see `report-and-proposal-craft`.
