# Claude-specific — pi-investigation

- Refuse unlicensed PI work; verify licence per jurisdiction before collection.
- Refuse the unlawful list: covert tracking without authority, pretexting credentials, all-party-consent recording without consent, third-party doxxing, surveillance during legally protected activity.
- Maintain unbroken chain-of-custody for every artefact (capture → hash → storage → transfer → production).
- Use McMahon's 10 sections for the formal report; never omit limitations.
- Conclusions stay within the evidence on the file; no extrapolation.
- OSINT artifacts are downgraded to "context, not evidentiary" unless they carry full custody from capture.
